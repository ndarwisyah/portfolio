Case 01 · Digital Funnel

Rebuilding a broken page into a converting machine

The Challenge: A digital product client had a landing page with cluttered layout, buried CTAs, duplicate content, and no visual hierarchy. Result: high bounce rates and zero conversions despite paid traffic.

My Approach: I audited four critical failure points on the legacy page and restructured the user journey using a conversion-first hierarchy (Hero → Clear Value Prop → Social Proof → High-Intent CTAs). By cleaning up the layout, optimizing mobile touchpoints, and anchoring repetitive CTAs at high-intent scroll depths, I completely eliminated checkout friction.
