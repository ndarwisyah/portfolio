The Objective

Designing for physical space requires flawless structural prep. For the Budi Madani event, the goal was to develop a curated, clean visual system across print and spatial touchpoints, operating within unyielding deadlines.

"Delivering cohesive branding under rapid high-volume turnaround, where real-world physical assets leave zero margin for digital correction."

The Workflow

Operational Strategy

02

Managed parallel pathways for multiple collateral pipelines simultaneously, achieving zero bottlenecks or structural dispatch delays.

03

Cross-Team Sync

Maintained tight iteration cycles with local operations and printing plants to map color profiles safely to physical inks.

04

Value Optimization

Strategized material selections, card weight thickness, and matte finishes to optimize event budgets without dropping premium aesthetic weight.
